The Hunger Games Series

Suzanne Collins

The Hunger Games is a series in which two teenagers from each of the thirteen districts are selected to participate in the annual hunger games. They will enter games with many obstacles and will fight until only one person remains. The leading character of the series is Katniss Everdeen, whose sister is chosen to be in the games. Katniss volunteers in place of her sister and the plot of each part of these series, revolves around her. The Hunger Games Series revolves around three books written by Suzanne Collins:

  • The Hunger games
  • Catching Fire
  • Mocking Jay
